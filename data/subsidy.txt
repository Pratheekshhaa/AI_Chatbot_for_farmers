Andhra Pradesh
Scheme: Talliki Vandanam Sector: Education / Social Welfare Description: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to mothers of school-going children to encourage attendance and reduce dropout rates. Benefit: ₹15,000 annually per eligible mother. Status: Rollout ordered for 2024-25 academic year; expenditure of ₹10,091 crore authorized.   

Scheme: Deepam-2 Sector: Energy / Social Welfare Description: Provision of free Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) cylinders to eligible households. Benefit: 3 free gas cylinders per year. Status: Active; part of the "Super Six" guarantees implemented in 2024-25.   

Scheme: Industrial Policy 2024-29 (Proposed) Sector: Industry Description: Focus on attracting investments with specific incentives for green energy and electronics. Benefit: Reimbursement of 100% stamp duty; electricity duty exemptions. Status: In drafting/consultation phase as of late 2024.

Scheme: AtmaNirbhar Bagwani Yojana (ANBY) Sector: Agriculture / Horticulture Description: Credit-linked subsidy scheme for cultivation of fruits like apple, kiwi, orange, etc. Benefit: 45% Government Subsidy, 45% Bank Loan, 10% Farmer contribution. No collateral for loans up to ₹1.60 lakh. Status: Active 2024-25.   

Scheme: AtmaNirbhar Pashu Palan Yojana (ANPPY) Sector: Animal Husbandry Description: Support for livestock farming (dairy, poultry, piggery). Benefit: 45% Front-ended capital subsidy; 45% Bank loan. Status: Active; applications invited for 2025-26.
